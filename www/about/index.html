About Gowanus Heights
home

Nestled in the valley between Brooklyn's Cobble Gardens and Park Slope neighborhoods everything you've heard about Gowanus Heights is true.

Purists will note that technically Gowanus Slope, sometimes referred to as Parkwanus, is the Eastern liminal space buffering Gowanus Heights and Park Slope and they are correct.

Gowanus Heights is a Permanent Autonomous Zone and proud to self-identify with Brooklyn Integer #18807771. Its sister neighborhood and soul-mate is San Francisco's mighty La Lengua.
