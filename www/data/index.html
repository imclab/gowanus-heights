Spatial data files for the boundaries of the Gowanus Heights neighborhood (and the Gowanus Canal) in Brooklyn, New York are provided free of charge in the GeoJSON and ESRI shapefile formats.

  • gowanus-heights.json (GeoJSON)
  • gowanus-heights.zip (Shapefile)
  • gowanus-canal.json (GeoJSON)
  • gowanus-canal.zip (Shapefile)

The data for the Gowanus Canal is courtesy the members of OpenStreetMap and licensed under the Creative Commons CCBYSA agreement.
